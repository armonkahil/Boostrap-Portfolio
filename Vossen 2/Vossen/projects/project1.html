Project Description

This is a simple hangman game written in Javascript. But by no means is the game simple.
I made this as difficult as possible by including 1,291 of the hardest words in the English dictionary.

The list I compiled, combined the most difficult words from International Spelling Bees,
GRE Vocabulary Study Guides, and Dr. Robert Heckendorn's List of Hard to Spell Words.

I chose a theme that I felt correctly related to my efforts in making this game difficult: The Saw Movie Series
